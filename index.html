About Us

RMK Gradba is a professional construction company with extensive experience in low-rise infrastructure projects. We specialize in the construction and maintenance of public and private infrastructure, delivering reliable and high-quality services across the region. With over 20 years of experience, our company has successfully completed more than 200 projects. Our highly qualified team of engineers, workers, and modern machinery enables us to execute projects of all scales quickly and efficiently.
