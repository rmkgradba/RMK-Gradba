Company Profile

Core Services and Capabilities

RMK Gradba specializes in a broad range of low-rise construction services tailored to meet diverse infrastructure needs. Our key capabilities include:

  • Design and installation of stormwater drainage and sanitary sewage systems.
  • High-quality concrete block paving for streets and sidewalks.
  • Comprehensive street construction and asphalt paving.
  • Installation and maintenance of water supply networks.
  • Additional civil works such as sidewalks, curbs, and retaining walls.
  • Project management ensuring timely and efficient execution.
